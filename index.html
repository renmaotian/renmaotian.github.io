Research Interests

My research focuses on developing innovative bioinformatics tools, pipelines, and machine learning algorithms to advance the understanding of microbial genomics and its applications in human health and the environment. I am particularly interested in leveraging my expertise in next-generation sequencing data analysis, comparative genomics, and machine learning to develop novel approaches for investigating microbial diversity, evolution, and pathogenesis.

PlasmidHunter

VBCG

ASAP 2
